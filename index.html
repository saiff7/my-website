FAQ

Questions? Answers.

Is ZdrawiAI free to use?

+

ZdrawiAI offers a free tier with core health monitoring and family connections. Premium features like advanced analytics and extended health history are available through an affordable subscription.

How accurate is the fall detection?

+

Our fall detection uses accelerometer data combined with pattern recognition—analyzing free-fall, impact force, and post-impact inactivity. It's designed to minimize false positives while catching real falls.

Can my family see all my health data?

+

No. ZdrawiAI is fully permission-based. You control exactly what data is shared and with whom. Every family member has granular control over their own health information visibility.

What devices does ZdrawiAI support?

+

ZdrawiAI is built with Flutter and supports both iOS and Android devices. It uses native health APIs and device sensors for accurate health monitoring across platforms.

Does the app drain my battery?

+

ZdrawiAI uses battery-conscious background processing. Fall detection and step counting run efficiently in the background without significant battery impact on modern devices.

How is my data protected?

+

We use Firebase Authentication with phone-based OTP, Supabase with row-level security, token-based API access, and HTTPS/TLS encryption. Your health data is never sold to third parties.
